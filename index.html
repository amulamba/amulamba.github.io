About Anna Mulamba

I am a designer based in Washington, DC. Although my background is in web and print design, I specialize in designing with the user in mind and have adopted UX strategies into every design project. My passions include UX/UI design, web design, print, branding, and content strategy. When I'm not designing I enjoy going out to eat with friends and family, watching movies, and filling up my passport with stamps.
